5 HTML Tags I've learned

<p>
Creates a paragraph.
<h1> to <h6>
Represents headings, with <h1> being the highest level (biggest) and <h6> the lowest (smallest).
<ul>
Creates an unordered list.
<img>
Embeds an image into the website.
<a>
Creates a hyperlink to another website.
